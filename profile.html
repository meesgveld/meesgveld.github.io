About Me

In my free time I really enjoy practicing all kind of sports. At the moment my goal is to finish a triathlon in 2026, so most of my active time is spent on training for that. But all these sports don't stop me from enjoying life, I love spending weekends with friends. Whether it’s at a festival, a terrace, or just enjoying a drink together.
